# How do I invite my team?

Add colleagues to DigitalCore so they can log time, view dashboards, and collaborate on engagements.

## How do I invite someone?

1. Navigate to **Settings → Team**
2. Click **+ Invite Member**
3. Enter their email address
4. Select their role
5. Click **Send Invitation**

The invitee receives an email with instructions to create their account.

## What roles are available?

| Role | Permissions |
|------|-------------|
| **Admin** | Full access: manage settings, users, all data |
| **Manager** | View all engagements, edit assigned engagements, view reports |
| **Member** | Log time, view assigned engagements only |
| **Viewer** | Read-only access to assigned engagements |

## How do I set cost rates?

Each team member needs an internal cost rate for margin calculations:

1. Go to **Settings → Team → [Member Name]**
2. Set **Internal Cost Rate** (hourly rate)
3. Optionally set **Bill Rate** for T&M billing

## How do I assign people to engagements?

1. Open the engagement
2. Click **Team** tab
3. Click **+ Add Member**
4. Select team members and their engagement role
5. Save changes
