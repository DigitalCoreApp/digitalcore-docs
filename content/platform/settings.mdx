# How do I configure settings?

The Settings area lets you customize DigitalCore for your organization.

## What settings are available?

**Organization Settings**
- Company profile
- Fiscal year
- Currency
- Time zones

**User Management**
- Invite/remove users
- Assign roles
- Set permissions

**Finance Settings**
- Cost rate defaults
- Billing configuration
- Revenue recognition rules

**Integrations**
- Connect external systems
- API keys
- Webhooks

**Notifications**
- Alert preferences
- Email settings
- In-app notification rules

## How do I access settings?

1. Click the gear icon in the sidebar, or
2. Navigate to **Settings** from the menu

## How do permissions work?

DigitalCore uses role-based access control (RBAC):

| Role | Access Level |
|------|--------------|
| **Admin** | Full access to all settings |
| **Manager** | Limited settings, no user management |
| **Member** | Personal settings only |
