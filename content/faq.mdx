# Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions and answers about DigitalCore.

## General

### What is DigitalCore?

DigitalCore is a service economics platform that helps professional services firms understand and optimize the profitability of their engagements. It provides real-time margin visibility, cost attribution, and AI-powered insights.

### Who is DigitalCore for?

DigitalCore is designed for:
- Professional services firms
- Consulting companies
- IT service providers
- Agencies
- Any organization delivering services to clients

### How is pricing calculated?

See our [Subscriptions](/docs/account/subscriptions) page for current plan details and pricing.

## Technical

### What integrations are available?

DigitalCore integrates with popular tools including time tracking (Harvest, Toggl), project management (Jira, Asana), accounting (QuickBooks, Xero), and more. See [Integrations](/docs/integrations) for the full list.

### Is there an API?

Yes! DigitalCore has a comprehensive REST API. See our [API documentation](/docs/api) to get started.

### Does DigitalCore support SSO?

SSO is available on Enterprise plans. We support Okta, Azure AD, and Google Workspace.

## Data & Security

### Where is my data stored?

Data is stored in secure, SOC 2 compliant data centers. Enterprise customers can choose data residency regions.

### Can I export my data?

Yes, you can export all your data at any time in CSV or JSON format from **Settings → Data → Export**.

### Is DigitalCore GDPR compliant?

Yes, DigitalCore is fully GDPR compliant. See our [Privacy Policy](https://www.digitalcore.app/privacy) for details.
