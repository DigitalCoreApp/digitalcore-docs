# Manage Account

Update your personal profile and account preferences.

## Profile settings

Update your profile at **Settings → Profile**:

- **Name** — display name across DigitalCore
- **Email** — login email and notifications
- **Avatar** — profile picture
- **Time zone** — for scheduling and displays
- **Language** — interface language

## Changing email

1. Go to **Settings → Profile**
2. Edit email address
3. Click **Save**
4. Verify new email via confirmation link

## Changing password

1. Go to **Settings → Security**
2. Click **Change Password**
3. Enter current password
4. Enter new password (twice)
5. Click **Update**

## Notification preferences

Control what notifications you receive:

| Type | Options |
|------|---------|
| **Email** | Daily digest, immediate, or off |
| **In-app** | Badge, popup, or off |
| **Slack** | If integrated |

Configure at **Settings → Notifications**.

## Deleting account

To delete your personal account:

1. Go to **Settings → Profile**
2. Scroll to **Delete Account**
3. Confirm with password

⚠️ This cannot be undone. Export your data first.
