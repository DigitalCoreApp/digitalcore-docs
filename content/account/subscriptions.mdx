# Subscriptions

View and manage your DigitalCore subscription.

## Plans

| Plan | Users | Features | Price |
|------|-------|----------|-------|
| **Starter** | Up to 5 | Core features | Free |
| **Professional** | Up to 25 | Full features | $99/mo |
| **Enterprise** | Unlimited | Advanced + support | Contact us |

## Current plan

View your current plan at **Settings → Subscription**.

You'll see:
- Plan name and features
- Billing cycle
- Next renewal date
- Current usage

## Upgrading

1. Go to **Settings → Subscription**
2. Click **Change Plan**
3. Select new plan
4. Confirm and pay

Upgrades take effect immediately. You're charged a prorated amount.

## Downgrading

1. Go to **Settings → Subscription**
2. Click **Change Plan**
3. Select new plan
4. Confirm

Downgrades take effect at the end of your billing period.

## Payment methods

Manage payment methods at **Settings → Billing → Payment Methods**.

We accept:
- Credit/debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex)
- ACH bank transfer (Enterprise)

## Invoices

Download invoices from **Settings → Billing → Invoices**.
