# Playbooks

Step-by-step guides for common scenarios and best practices in DigitalCore.

## What are playbooks?

Playbooks are comprehensive guides that walk you through specific processes, decisions, or challenges you might face when managing service economics.

Each playbook includes:
- **Context** — when and why to use it
- **Steps** — detailed instructions
- **Examples** — real-world scenarios
- **Tips** — best practices and pitfalls

## Available playbooks

Playbooks are dynamically loaded from DigitalCore. Use the AI assistant to access them:

> "Show me the playbook for improving engagement margins"

> "What playbook helps with client negotiations?"

## Playbook categories

**Financial**
- Margin improvement
- Pricing optimization
- Cost reduction

**Operational**
- Engagement kickoff
- Resource allocation
- Project recovery

**Strategic**
- Portfolio optimization
- Service design
- Client retention

## Using playbooks

1. Ask the AI assistant for a relevant playbook
2. Review the context to confirm it applies
3. Follow the steps in order
4. Adapt to your specific situation
